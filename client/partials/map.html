There's a lot going on in the world today. You want to see the world, but you want to be safe. Danger zones was built for you. Danger zones visually represents travel advisories issued by the U.S. Sate Department. Armed with the information presented here, travelers can decide whether trips to highlighted countries are worth the risk.
  • Countries highlighted in yellow have current alerts. They are temporarily unsafe for travel due to transient conditions such as extreme weather conditions or elections in politically unstable regions.
  • Countries highlighted in red have current warnings, and pose a serious and ongoing threat to traveler safety, including, but not limited to: piracy, sexual violence, and terrorist activity.
  • Click on a highlighted country to read the text of the travel alert or warning. In addition, links are provided to pertinent news stories regarding the situation in the country in question.
  • For more information, or to contact the U.S State Department, visit them here: U.S. State Department Travel
